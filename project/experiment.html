Experiment 1_Manual Electric Power Generator


The aim of this experiment is to be able to observe how mechanical energy is transformed into electrical energy. From human movements we can create energy thanks to magnetic induction, Faraday's Law. This law told relates the magnitude of the electromotive force induced in a loop to the change in magnetic flux through the loop.

But do you think we could use our own movements to create renewable energy?
