Food Degradation

Link to the presentation

The evolution of food towards its degradation is used as a metaphor for life itself. It explores the human behaviour towards nature, with the capitalist system as the catalyst.
The focus is on the exploitation of food systems and the generated waste that feeds the same. The speculation explores not only the benefit of the privilege but also the aspect of us Homo sapiens being a speciesist entity in this ecosystem.
